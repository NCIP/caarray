We invite the community to try out the Pre-Release of caArray 2.0!



To enter test data into the pre-release system, please Register for an account and you will be provided with a password to login to the system. You may Browse and Search Public data in the system anonymously, but do note there is limited data in the pre-release system. Please note that data and accounts for the caArray 2.0 Pre-Release will not be migrated to the caArray 2.0 Production system - you will need to re-register once caArray 2.0 is officially released.

The application is still under active development and you may encounter some bugs and/or performance issues in this Pre-Release. We welcome your feedback on any issues encountered and ask that you submit these issues to our Community Change Request tracker at: https://gforge.nci.nih.gov/tracker/?atid=1339&group_id=305&func=browse. You may review defects that have been identified by our internal Quality Assurance team at https://gforge.nci.nih.gov/tracker/?atid=1344&group_id=305&func=browse.

To try the Pre-Release, click on the button, GO!
